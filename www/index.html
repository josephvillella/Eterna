Kindly Note...

Our goal is to ensure you completely enjoy your time with us.  You are the main ingredient in our services.

For your visit with us, please arrive 15 minutes prior to your appointment. This will allot enough time for a spa tour, changing into spa attire, and a consultation with your Spa Therapist. If you desire extra time to relax and unwind, you are welcomed to come earlier and enjoy a cup of Aveda 100% Organic Comforting Tea as you settle in.

Your privacy and comfort are of the utmost importance to us. Spa treatments require various levels of undress. We assure our Spa Therapists are trained in appropriate methods of draping and we offer discreet spa robes and attire for your comfort. Although your therapist will discuss with you a complete overview of the treatment you will experience, please feel free to ask any appropriate questions or make special requests to ensure your own comfort level is achieved.

PARENTAL COURTESY / COURTESY OF OTHERS:

A Spa environment is one that is relaxing and serene. We kindly ask that parents please make child care arrangements prior to your spa appointments -unless a child is receiving a spa treatment.  Child care will not be available. 

We ask that all guests please turn off cell phones, pagers, computers and any other devices that may interfere with the serenity of fellow guests.

HEALTH CONCERNS:

Please advise us of any medical conditions or health related issues that may interfere with your treatment.

BOOKINGS AND CANCELLATIONS:

We ask that you give us 24 hours notice if you need to cancel your appointment.

Thank You!
